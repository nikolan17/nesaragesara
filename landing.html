FAQ

What is NESARA? ➕
NESARA stands for the National Economic Security and Recovery Act, a set of reform proposals drafted in the 1990s by Harvey Francis Barnard. Key ideas included:
  • Abolishing compound interest on loans
  • Returning to a bimetallic (gold/silver) currency standard
  • Eliminating the federal income tax in favor of a national sales tax
Barnard self-published a book and sent it to Congress, but it was never introduced as law.
What is GESARA? ➕
GESARA stands for the Global Economic Security and Reformation Act. Supporters claim NESARA’s supposed reforms were expanded worldwide to create a global system of economic reset, debt forgiveness, and financial equality.
How did NESARA turn into a conspiracy theory? ➕
In the late 1990s and early 2000s, online communities promoted the idea that NESARA was secretly passed but suppressed by powerful interests. Shaini Goodwin (“Dove of Oneness”) became a prominent promoter, claiming:
  • NESARA was real but hidden
  • It would wipe out personal debts, end income tax, and redistribute wealth
  • “Dark forces” were blocking its implementation
These claims spread online and later intertwined with other narratives, including UFO and QAnon-adjacent themes.
What do believers say NESARA/GESARA will do? ➕
Followers often claim the acts would:
  • Cancel all debts (personal, national, and global)
  • Eliminate income tax and introduce a flat or sales tax
  • Release suppressed technologies (e.g., “free energy”)
  • End wars and establish world peace
  • Distribute wealth more equally worldwide
  • Reveal hidden truths about extraterrestrials and government corruption
