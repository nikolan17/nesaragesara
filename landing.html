Frequently Asked Questions

What is NESARA? ➕
NESARA stands for the National Economic Security and Recovery Act, a set of reform proposals drafted in the 1990s by Harvey Francis Barnard. Key ideas included:
  • Abolishing compound interest on loans
  • Returning to a bimetallic (gold/silver) currency standard
  • Eliminating the federal income tax in favor of a national sales tax
Barnard self-published a book and sent it to Congress, but it was never introduced as law.
What is GESARA? ➕
GESARA stands for the Global Economic Security and Reformation Act. Supporters claim NESARA’s supposed reforms were expanded worldwide to create a global system of economic reset, debt forgiveness, and financial equality.
